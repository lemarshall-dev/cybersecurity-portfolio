## Objectives

- I will also understand what makes each type suited to its purpose.

## Tools

## What I did

- Finished with a 5 question quiz, pass mark 4/5.

## What I learned
- I learned that computers aren't all just laptops and desktops, there are many things which are considered computers even if they don't look it.
- IoT Devices (Internet of things) are devices which normally have a singular purpose and connect to the network, for example, Smart bulbs, Thermostats, fitness trackers etc.
- Each type of computer have their own purpose and there's no single "best" computer that tackles all tasks. For example, workstations will be better for long labour intensive tasks over a laptop
  which is built for portable computing like answering emails, making video calls etc.
